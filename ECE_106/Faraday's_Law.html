Induced Voltage

What happens when B is changing with time?

If linkage changes with time, there is an induced voltage across the circuit.

Assume the circuit is a battery. TIP: Always assume dl is counterclockwise and ds is up. If the assumption is wrong, the answer will simply appear negative, indicating that the direction was opposite of what was assumed.
